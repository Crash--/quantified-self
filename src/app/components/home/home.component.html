Welcome to Quantified Self v{{ appVersion }}

 The one and only tool to analyse your data from any fitness device and not only Hosted on Google App Engine & FireBase, made with Angular Source code:

Quantified Self is a tool for importing viewing and comparing tcx, fit and json files from activity trackers and smart-watches such as Garmin, Suunto, Polar etc.

Currently the support is limit to the main formats: GPX, TCX, FIT and JSON*

*JSON is for specific services while GPX, TCX, FIT should be compatible with the more common services, such as Strava, Movescount, Garmin, Polar and any other service that supports the above exports.
